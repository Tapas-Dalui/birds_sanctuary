Vedanthangal Bird Sanctuary, Tamil Nadu

This is one of the smallest bird sanctuaries in India. The literal meaning of Vedanthangal sanctuary is “hamlet of the hunters.” It is spread on 30 acres of land. Nevertheless, in terms of diversity of the species of the birds, it is one of the rarest sanctuaries. It comprises more than 26 rare species of birds and more than 40, 000 migration birds visit here during the winter season. It is one of the oldest water bird sanctuaries in India.

  • Types of birds found here are: Grey Wagtails, Garganeys, Spoonbills, Blue-Winged Teals, Pintails, Common Sandpipers, Herons, and others
  • Ideal time to visit: October to January
  • Timing: 06:00 A.M. to 06:00 P.M.
  • Entry price: INR 5 per to 25
